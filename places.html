Top Places to Visit

Buckingham Palace

Both a functioning royal palace and the Queen's official residence in London. Be sure to attend the Changing of the Guard ceremony.

  • Opening Hours: 9:30 AM to 7:30 PM
  • Entry Price: Adult: £35.00, 18-24: £22.50, Child: £17.50
  • Nearest Stop: Victoria, Green Park, St. James's Park.
View on Google Maps

The British Museum

An official organisation devoted to art, culture, and human history. The Rosetta Stone's home.

  • Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM Daily.
  • Entry Price: Free
  • Nearest Stop: Holborn, Tottenham Court Road.
View on Google Maps

The Shard

A 95-story skyscraper that offers 360-degree city views from its viewing platforms.

  • Opening Hours: Thusday 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Friday 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Saturday 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Sunday 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM
  • Entry Price: GA ticket starting from £28.50,under 3 for free
  • Nearest Stop: London Bridge
View on Google Maps

The Tower of London

On the north bank of the Thames River stands a historic castle, where England's Crown Jewels are kept.

  • Opening Hours: 10:00 AM to 5:30 PM
  • Entry Price: £29.90
  • Nearest Stop: Tower Hill
View on Google Maps
