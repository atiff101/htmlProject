Welcome to London

The capital of England and the United Kingdom, London, has a Roman past and is a city in the twenty-first century. Its main attractions are Westminster Abbey, the location of the coronations of British monarchs, the majestic Houses of Parliament, and the famous "Big Ben" clock tower. The London Eye observation wheel offers sweeping views of the city and the South Bank cultural area across the Thames River.
